FAQ

Quick answers about Puzzle Nexus Hub and the standalone games.

Is Puzzle Nexus Hub free?

Yes. It’s free to download on Google Play. Some titles may include ads and optional in-app purchases.

Does Puzzle Nexus Hub work offline?

Yes. Most puzzles can be played offline without an internet connection. Some features like ads or updates may require internet access.

Which games are included in the Hub?

Sudoku, Nonogram, Solitaire, Water Sort, Word Search, Minesweeper — and more games are planned over time.

Does the game save my progress?

Yes. Your puzzle progress is saved locally on your device so you can continue where you left off.

Are there ads in the game?

Puzzle Nexus Hub is free to download and may include ads. Ads help support ongoing updates and new puzzle modes.

Can I remove ads?

Some versions may offer an in-app purchase to remove ads. Check the in-app store inside the game for availability.

Why use Puzzle Nexus Hub instead of separate apps?

Puzzle Nexus Hub brings multiple puzzle games into one clean app, so you don’t need multiple downloads. Switch between puzzles anytime in one place.

How do I contact support?

Email ajviogames@gmail.com and include your device model + Android version if it’s a bug report.
