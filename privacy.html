Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Ayvio Games (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use our Android apps, including Puzzle Nexus Hub and our standalone puzzle games.

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, email: ajviogames@gmail.com

Overview

Our apps are designed to be calm, simple, and easy to use. Some apps are ad-supported and may offer optional in-app purchases. We do not require an account to play.

Information that may be processed

Depending on the app and your device settings, the following types of information may be processed:

  • Device information (device model, OS version, language, region).
  • Diagnostics (crash logs, performance data, error reports).
  • Advertising data (ad impressions/clicks and identifiers used for ads, where available).
  • Purchase information (purchases are processed by Google Play; we receive confirmation to unlock items).
  • Gameplay events (basic events to improve features and stability).

How we use information

  • To operate the apps and provide gameplay features.
  • To fix bugs, prevent crashes, and improve performance.
  • To understand which features are useful and improve the experience.
  • To show ads in ad-supported apps and measure ad performance.
  • To process and restore purchases (when applicable).

Third-party services

Our apps may use third-party services that process information to provide analytics, diagnostics, ads, or purchases. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

  • Google Play Services (distribution, licensing, purchase processing)
  • Google AdMob (ads in ad-supported apps)
  • Analytics / diagnostics (performance and stability)

Ads & personalization

Ads may be contextual or personalized depending on your device settings, region, and the ad network configuration. You can manage ad personalization from your Google/Android settings.

Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes described above (for example, stability diagnostics or aggregated analytics). Third-party services may retain information according to their own policies.

Children’s privacy

Our apps are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is provided by necessary third-party services (such as ads delivery or diagnostics). If you believe personal information was provided to us directly, contact us and we will address it.

Your choices

  • You can stop all collection by uninstalling the app.
  • You can control certain permissions and privacy settings in Android system settings.
  • You can manage ad personalization in your Google/Android settings.
  • You may request deletion of data we control by emailing ajviogames@gmail.com.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information processed by our apps. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we aim to reduce risk and protect user data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated date.
